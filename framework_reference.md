<h1>Extended The Rohan Model CGF Framework</h1>
<b>This respository extends the Rohan Model & CGF framework originality developed by Dr. Nisha Rawindaran Nair. 
The Original research can be found Rawindaran, N., Jayal, A. and Prakash, E., 2025. Cybersecurity Framework: 
Addressing Resiliency in Welsh SMEs for Digital Transformation and Industry 5.0. Journal of Cybersecurity and Privacy, 5(2), p.17. 
https://www.mdpi.com/2624-800X/5/2/17 (Accessed: 16/05/2025).</b>
 
<b>This project extends the Rohan Model / CGF by 
converting conceptual governance and maturity domains into a measurable, repeatable cyber hygiene assessment.
I designed the control mapping from survey questions to governance domains, implemented a normalised maturity scoring model, 
and added stage-based recommendations to make the framework actionable for startups at different growth stages. 
I also integrated classification and anomaly detection to support control assurance-style insights and highlight inconsistent or unusual 
posture patterns, delivering the full capability as an interactive Streamlit scorecard.</b>
